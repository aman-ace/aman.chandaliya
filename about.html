About

Hi, I'm Aman.

A finance writer and analyst — curious about everything from equity markets to fiscal policy, and committed to clear, well-sourced thinking.

Background

I'm an early-career finance professional graduating soon, with a background spanning markets, corporate finance, and public-sector finance. I read widely — The Wall Street Journal, the Financial Times, The Economist — and I write to make complex financial ideas accessible.

What I'm interested in

  • Equity research, valuation, and investment frameworks
  • Corporate finance: capital structure, M&A, and capital allocation
  • Macro and policy finance — central banks, fiscal policy, regulation
  • Personal finance and long-term wealth building
  • Financial data, modeling, and clear visualization

How I work

I lean on primary sources — company filings, central bank publications, statutory documentation, and peer-reviewed research from databases like Web of Science and Business Source Complete. Citations follow Chicago style. The goal is always: sourced, structured, and useful.

Currently

Actively pursuing roles in equity research, financial analysis, and finance journalism. I'm also building a portfolio of writing aimed at financial news outlets and academic platforms.
