Project

State Budget Tracker

By Aman Chandaliya · 2026

Overview

The State Budget Tracker is a comparative dataset and visualization project covering Connecticut, Florida, and Wyoming. It pulls statutory budget reports and reformats them into a single, navigable schema.

Stack & Sources

Python · Pandas · CSV · static HTML

Status

In active development

Outcome

Reduces the time required to produce comparative state-budget research from days to hours.
