Valuation Isn't a Formula — It's a Story

By Aman Chandaliya · April 2026

A DCF model isn't an oracle. It's a discipline. It forces you to write down a story about a business — its growth, its margins, its reinvestment, its end state — and check whether that story is internally consistent. When a model produces a number you don't believe, the answer is rarely 'tweak the WACC.' The answer is almost always: revise the story.
