Thinking in Base Rates

By Aman Chandaliya · May 2026

Most investing mistakes aren't analytical. They're failures to anchor on the base rate — the reference class of what usually happens. Before asking 'is this company a great investment,' it helps to ask: what fraction of companies that look like this one have actually compounded capital over a decade? Base-rate thinking won't tell you the answer, but it tells you how confident you should be in any answer. That alone is worth a great deal.
