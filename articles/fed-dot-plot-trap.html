The Dot Plot Trap

By Aman Chandaliya · March 2026

The Fed's Summary of Economic Projections is genuinely informative — about the committee. It tells you how policymakers see the world right now, given current data. It is not a forecast of where rates will go, and treating it as one has cost market participants billions over the past two decades. Read the dot plot to understand the Fed; read the data to understand the path.
