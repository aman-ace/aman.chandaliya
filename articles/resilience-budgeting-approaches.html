Three Approaches to Resilience Budgeting

By Aman Chandaliya · April 2026

Florida emphasizes recurring resilience-linked appropriations tied to coastal exposure. Connecticut leans on dedicated risk-disclosure requirements within its capital budget. Wyoming, by contrast, treats resilience largely through general capital reserves rather than line-item designations. Comparing these three approaches reveals that statutory architecture, not just funding levels, shapes resilience outcomes.
