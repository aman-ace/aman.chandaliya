Reading State Budgets Like a Researcher

By Aman Chandaliya · March 2026

Start with the statute, not the spreadsheet. Identify the controlling budget law, then map each major report — revenue estimate, executive proposal, enacted budget, comprehensive annual financial report — back to its statutory basis. From there, the numbers stop feeling arbitrary and start telling a structured, traceable story.
